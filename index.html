About

From mathematical structure to financial intelligence.

Bill Sun works at the intersection of AI research, stochastic decision-making, and capital allocation. The recurring question is how to design systems that can reason under uncertainty and still survive the feedback loops of the real world.

That spans architecture choices, sparse representations, better optimization, reinforcement learning, and portfolio-level action. The applied target is unusually concrete: build the best AI for trading and investing.

Identity

Researcher-first, founder-shaped, with one foot in frontier model design and one foot in competitive financial systems.

Operating thesis

Strong financial AI requires more than prediction. It requires research loops, monetization logic, portfolio discipline, and capital-aware agents.

Research

Five themes that define the work.

01

Model architecture

Transformer-era architecture questions, attention mechanisms, and scaling behavior that matter in actual training systems.

02

Representation learning

Sparse high-dimensional structure, unsupervised learning, Mixture of Experts style specialization, and iterative neural models.

03

Stochastic optimization

Better SGD behavior, optimization under noise, and methods that remain stable at scale instead of only looking elegant on paper.

04

Decision-making under uncertainty

Reinforcement learning, stochastic control, portfolio optimization, and robust action selection in delayed-feedback environments.

05

AI for markets

Building systems that can research, trade, allocate capital, and operate as machine-native participants in financial markets.
